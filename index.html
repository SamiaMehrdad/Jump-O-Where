How to play Jump-O-Where

Jump one peg over another into an empty hole on the other side, will remove the jumped peg from the board. Pegs cannot jump diagonally, nor over more than one peg at a time. Reduce pegs to one to pass each one of 20 levels, from easy to difficult.
Click on any peg and then highlighted hole, if there is any, to do jumping.
